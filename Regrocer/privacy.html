Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

Regrocer is designed with your privacy in mind. Your data is stored locally on your device and synced through your personal iCloud account. We do not operate any external servers, and we never collect, sell, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data We Store

Regrocer stores the following data on your device and in your iCloud account:

  • Grocery list contents (item names, quantities, prices, categories)
  • Pantry inventory (items, quantities, storage locations, expiration dates)
  • Purchase history (items checked off, prices, dates)
  • Your profile information (nickname, avatar color and symbol)
  • App preferences and settings

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you are signed in to iCloud, your data is automatically synced to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure — your data is never sent to any other server.

Shared Lists

When you share a grocery list with household members, that list's data is shared through iCloud's sharing infrastructure. Only people you explicitly invite can access the shared list. You can revoke access at any time.

No Tracking or Analytics

Regrocer does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or behavioral information. There are no cookies, no fingerprinting, and no third-party scripts.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. Your grocery lists, pantry inventory, and personal information are yours alone.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by removing the app from your device. If you use iCloud sync, you can also delete your data from iCloud through your device's Settings under iCloud Storage.

Children's Privacy

Regrocer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general household use and does not target children specifically.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at support@thomasaj.com.
